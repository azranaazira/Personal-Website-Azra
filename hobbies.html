Travelling

Travelling give a million new experiences!!

I enjoy travelling to different locations with my family. Besides spending time with them, I gained a variety of new experiences. Experience is something that must be gained and cannot be purchased. There are numerous new experiences to be had. For example, when I travelled to Sabah in 2018, I was able to board a plane for the first time. In addition, I was able to ride a boat to Manukan and Sapi Islands while there.

Watching Korean Drama

Dramas teaches countless valuable lessons that are worthwhile!

I am somewhat different from my friends who prefer to watch English and Chinese dramas. Moreover, I am familiar with Korean dramas because all of my sisters enjoy music and dramas from there. Korean dramas are more interesting and diverse than Malaysian dramas that mainly focus on romance. I recommend the drama Reply 1988 to my friends because the storyline is excellent and contains valuable lessons.
